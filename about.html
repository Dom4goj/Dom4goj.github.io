About Me

I am Domagoj Hajduković. A passionate and innovative junior UX/UI Designer with a strong foundation in graphic design. My journey began at the School of Applied Arts and Design Osijek, where I honed my artistic skills and developed a keen eye for aesthetics. Recently, I expanded my knowledge by successfully completing a UX/UI Design course at Algebra University College.

With a solid background in graphic design, I have spent two years in the industry, immersing myself in the world of visual communication. This experience has enabled me to seamlessly merge creativity with functionality, producing designs that captivate audiences while delivering meaningful user experiences.

My expertise extends beyond visuals. I have a profound appreciation for problem-solving and a understanding of user thoughts. Equipped with knowledge of practiced UX methods, I approach each project with a user-centric mindset, ensuring that the end product resonates with its intended audience.

While my strengths lie in the realm of visuals, I am humbly setting foot in the expansive field of front-end development. I am currently in the early stages of my learning journey and am excited to embrace the challenges that lie ahead. My curiosity and determination drive me to continuously enhance my front-end skills, contributing a dynamic edge to my design process.

One of my proudest achievements is the culmination of my education and skill set into a comprehensive portfolio website. This platform not only showcases my work but also exemplifies my dedication to design excellence and constant improvement.

Collaboration fuels my creativity, and I am excited about the prospect of joining a team of fellow designers and developers. I have a unique ability to identify user experience opportunities in everyday life, and I relish brainstorming sessions with friends to solve common pain points of products we use daily.

In my view, design is a powerful tool that can transform the way people interact with the world, and I am excited to continue pushing boundaries and creating impactful, user-centric experiences.
